# Privacy Policy for OmniAI

**Last Updated: July 16, 2026**

## Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Welcome to OmniAI! Your privacy is our top priority. This app is designed to be a simple, secure portal to third-party web services. We want to be completely transparent about what our app does and, more importantly, what it does not do.

**Our core principle is simple: We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information for our own purposes.**

## 1. No User Accounts or Data Collection by OmniAI

OmniAI does not have its own login or account system. We do not ask for your name, email address, or any other personal details. The app functions as a specialized browser, and any account you log into is with a third-party service (like ChatGPT, for example) that is loaded within the app's web view.

We do not collect or have access to:
* Your login credentials (passwords) for any third-party service.
* The content you create, upload, or share on those services.
* Your browsing history within the web view.

## 2. Third-Party AI Services

OmniAI provides access to over 25+ third-party AI services (ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Claude, DeepSeek, Perplexity, and others) through its built-in web view. **These services are independent third parties, not operated or controlled by OmniAI.**

When you use these services through OmniAI:
- You are interacting directly with the third-party service's website.
- Any data you submit (text, images, files, voice) is sent to that third-party service.
- Each service has its own privacy policy, terms of service, and data handling practices.
- We encourage you to review the privacy policy of each service you use.

**OmniAI does not transmit, store, or process your conversations or generated content.** The web view loads the service's website directly, just like any browser would.

## 3. Persistent Login and Session Management

To provide a seamless and convenient experience, OmniAI offers a persistent login feature. This means that when you log into a service (like Google for ChatGPT), we will help you stay logged in for your next session. Here's how it works:

* **Session Cookies**: We save the session cookies from the websites you log into. These cookies are small pieces of data that the website uses to recognize you and keep you logged in. They are stored securely on your device.
* **OAuth Tokens**: For services that use OAuth for authentication, we may store the authentication tokens on your device.

**We never store your password.** We only store the session information that allows you to avoid logging in every time you open the app. You have full control over this feature and can clear your session data at any time from the app's settings.

## 4. Downloads (AI-Generated Images, Videos, and Files)

OmniAI allows you to download AI-generated content (images, videos, and other files) from third-party services. Here's how downloads work:

- Files are saved to your device's public storage (Pictures, Movies, or Downloads folder) using Android's standard MediaStore API.
- On Android 10+ (API 29+), no storage permission is required for saving downloads.
- You can manage or delete downloaded files through your device's file manager or gallery app at any time.
- OmniAI does not access, copy, or transmit downloaded content anywhere — it stays on your device.

## 5. Permissions We Request

To provide a rich web experience, our Android application requests a limited set of permissions. Here's why we need them:

* **`INTERNET` and `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`**: Essential for the app to connect to the internet.
* **`CAMERA`**: Required so that websites you visit can ask for camera access for features like video calls or uploading a profile picture. The app only enables the website to request access, which you can approve or deny.
* **`RECORD_AUDIO`**: Required so that websites can ask for microphone access for voice commands or audio calls.
* **`MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS`**: Allows the app to adjust audio settings for media playback or calls initiated by the website.
* **`READ_MEDIA_IMAGES`, `READ_MEDIA_VIDEO`, `READ_MEDIA_AUDIO`** (Android 13+): Allow you to upload files to the websites you visit. The app only helps you select a file when a website requests it.
* **`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`** (Android 13+): Used to show download progress notifications.
* **`WAKE_LOCK`**: Prevents the screen from turning off during video playback.
* **`VIBRATE`**: Provides haptic feedback for notifications or actions initiated by the website.

**No storage permissions are required for downloading AI-generated content.** On Android 10+, downloads use the MediaStore API which does not require `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` or `MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`.

## 6. Advertising

OmniAI uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID) and usage data to serve relevant ads. This data is handled by Google in accordance with their privacy policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device's Google settings.

## 7. Data Security

All session information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. Data transferred between the app and the websites you visit is encrypted in transit (HTTPS).

## 8. Children's Privacy

OmniAI is rated for ages 3+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The third-party AI services accessible through the app have their own age restrictions and privacy practices.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we ever change our app's functionality in a way that impacts your privacy, we will update this policy to reflect those changes.
